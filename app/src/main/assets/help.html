Welcome!

How to use:

Home Screen:

  • Want to track the weather of a new location? Its easy! Click on the + button to navigate to Add Location Screen and bookmark a new location.
  • All set! Click on a location to navigate to Weather Forecast Screen and view it's weather forecast
  • No longer want to track a location? Swipe left a location to remove it from your bookmarks. No worries you can undo instantly!
  • Find your location fast by typing on the search filter. Click on the search icon to apply the fitler!
  • Click on the Settings icon to navigate to the Settings Screen and Help Screen. Being here means you already found it !

Add Location Screen:

  • Find the location you want to bookmark by dropping the pin on the Map.
  • Adjust the location name if you wish by typing a new name.
  • Click the ν button to complete and add the location.

Weather Forecast Screen:

  • Click the < > button to navigate the days and view the complete weather forecast. Hint: it is is easier if you swipe left and right!

Settings Screen:

  • Switch between metric or imperial system with one click.
  • Are all your bookmarked locations useless by now? Delete them all at once. Careful! There is not an undo option.

Have a great day!

(With great weather)
