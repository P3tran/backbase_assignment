Smooth Weather

How to use:

Home Screen:

  • Click on the + button to navigate to Add Location Screen and bookmark a new location.
  • Click on a location to navigate to Weather Forecast Screen and view the weather forecast for the desired location.
  • Swipe a location on the right to remove it from your bookmarks. No worries you can undo the action!
  • Click on the Settings icon to navigate to Settings Screen and to Help Screen. Great job being here means you found it !

Add Location Screen:

  • Find the location you want to bookmark by dropping the pin.
  • Adjust the location name if you wish by typing a new name.

Weather Forecast Screen:
