Welcome!

How to use:

Home Screen:

  • Want to track the weather of a location? Its easy! Click on the + button to navigate to Add Location Screen and bookmark a new location.
  • All set! Click on a location to navigate to Weather Forecast Screen and view the weather forecast for the desired location.
  • No longer want to track a location? Swipe a location on the right to remove it from your bookmarks. No worries you can undo the action!
  • Find your location fast by typing on the search filter and click the search icon to find your location faster!
  • Click on the Settings icon to navigate to Settings Screen and to Help Screen. Great job being here means you found it !

Add Location Screen:

  • Find the location you want to bookmark by dropping the pin on the Map.
  • Adjust the location name if you wish by typing a new name.
  • Click theν button to add the location.

Weather Forecast Screen:

  • Click the < > button to navigate the days and view the complete weather forecast. Hint: it is is easier if you swipe left and right!

Settings Screen:

  • Swich between metric or imperial system.
  • Are all your bookmarked locations useless by now? Delete them all at once. Careful! There is not an undo option.

Have a great day!

(With great weather)
